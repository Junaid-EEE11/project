I am an Electrical Engineer with professional experience in power distribution systems and substation operations at Dhaka Power Distribution Company Ltd. (DPDC). To strengthen my analytical and research capabilities, I pursued a Professional Master's degree in Applied Statistics and Data Science.

My interests lie at the intersection of Electrical Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. I am particularly interested in machine learning applications for energy systems, predictive maintenance, time series forecasting, smart grid analytics, and sustainable energy optimization.

My long-term goal is to pursue advanced research and contribute to the development of intelligent and sustainable energy systems.
