I am an Electrical Engineer with professional experience in power-distribution systems and substation operations at Dhaka Power Distribution Company Ltd. (DPDC). I also completed a Professional Master's in Applied Statistics and Data Science at Jahangirnagar University.

My interests lie at the intersection of electrical engineering, artificial intelligence, and data science. I am particularly interested in machine-learning applications for energy systems, predictive maintenance, time-series forecasting, smart-grid analytics, and sustainable energy optimization.

This site documents my engineering work, data-science projects, and developing research interests.
